Creative Brief

Part 3/3

Problem:

In a contemporary world where memes and emojis rule the digital conversation, new digital artists may find few platforms to promote and share their work. Most sticker platforms focus on big-name creators, leaving smaller artists without an easy way to reach audiences or build a following.

Audience:

It targets those who text every day and want fun, creative ways to express themselves beyond emojis. It also caters to young digital artists seeking a platform to share their work, gain followers, and connect with fans.

Competition:

Sticker packs on apps like WhatsApp, Telegram, and LINE already give users plenty of options, but they mostly feature pre-made or brand-driven sets. Unlike those, this site focuses on giving independent artists a spotlight.

Key Messages:

This site is a creative hub where digital stickers become a new way to communicate. It empowers artists to share their work, helps users find unique styles that match their personality, and turns everyday texting into something more expressive.

Call To Action:

Somthing like: Join the site to download free stickers, follow your favorite artists, and share their work with friends. Artists can upload their own packs and grow a community around their designs.

How to Measure Success:

Success can be measured by the number of active users downloading stickers, the growth of artist sign-ups, and how often people return to the site.
